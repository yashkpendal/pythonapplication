Wheat

Price: ₹50
  • Whole wheat light brown coloured spindle-shaped, tough, starchy and rich-tasting husked kernels of the wheat crop.
  • Rich in fiber, vitamins like Vitamin B, Vitamin C and Vitamin E.
  • Wheat is a part of our each day diet.
Family: Poaceae
Categories: Grain
Share Link:
  • Methodology

There are various things to keep in mind like soil texture, fertility,etc while cultivating wheat.
They are as follows:

  • - Choose a soil texture which is loamy and has moderate water holding
  • - Deep ploughing followed by 2-3 light ploughing and planking
  • - For overall lifecycle wheat requires an average of 50 kg Nitrogen, 25 kg Phosphorus and 12 kg Potash
  • - Wheat grows best between 21 째C and 26 째C although can withstand temperature between 3.5 째C and 35 째C
  • - Many varities of wheat are available you can choose DBW 17, HD 2851 or HD 2932
  • - First irrigation must be done after 20 to 25 days after planting the seeds and followed by 20 days gap
  • - When the moisture content in the wheat reaches to about 25 - 30%, then the wheat is ready to be harvested.
  • All the measurements are for 1 acre of land and can varying depending upon sowing method.
